About Us

In 1999 J & M Boatlift & Repair Inc. was born. It started with a small crew of 2 guys just trying to make their mark into the Marine Construction Industry and today it is a thriving company with multiple employees, office staff, and a 10,000 sq. ft. building with ¾ of an acre of property in downtown Naples. J & M prides itself on superior quality workmanship and a reputation of completing jobs on schedule and to perfection. Our projects include everything from small repairs and services to single family docks and all the way up to large condominium and commercial projects. No job is too small or too big for J & M. The owner of J & M originally got into the business by working for and became the Manager of the original Marine Lumber Supply here in Naples. With the experience and knowledge of the materials that were being used in the marine construction industry he then started to get his feet wet with understanding and building with other marine contractors on the side until he felt it was time to step out on his own. J & M is now one of the premier Marine Contractors in the Naples area. They are fully licensed in both Collier and Lee County and carry all the proper insurance including but not limited to all the state and federal workers compensation and longshoreman's.

Licensed and insured in Collier County and Lee County. J&M Boatlift and Repair carries all proper insurance to protect both employees and customers including but not limited to USL&H Insurance.
