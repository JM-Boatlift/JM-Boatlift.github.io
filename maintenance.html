Boat Lift Maintenance Service

  1. Visually inspect complete lift (which includes but not limited to: belts, pulleys, gearboxes, cradle beams and brackets, cables, top beams, and all welded areas)
  2. Grease all zirk fittings on lift
  3. Inspect and rewrap cables if needed
  4. Check all hardware and aluminum on lift for corrosion or wear.
  5. Adjust autostop if needed or requested.
  6. Level lift as needed.
